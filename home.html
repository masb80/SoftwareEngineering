The online voting system has been developed for the people to be able to vote online, whether it be a parliamentary or student union elections. The election committee decides to create the election, initialize the name and checks if they meet the eligibility criteria for the specific election. The candidate must register himself with the election committee and declare his eligibility and position: presidential or district. The Election Commission generates a revised list of candidates up for voting based on the rules of the particular elections. The election commission also decides on the duration and directions of the election. The voters must go online and register himself for the election. To register the voters must meet the eligibility criteria set up for him by Election Commission. The voter can log in to his account and fetch all the details regarding the elections, i.e. the list of candidates, directions, duration of election and rounds of elections. Finally, the votes are counted and the committee shall declare a winner. The declared results can be seen by the voter, election committee and candidate.
